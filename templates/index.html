Scrabble Trainer

About

Scrabble is a very challenging game, needing a variety of skill sets such as:

- Vocabulary Knowledge

Players need a broad and deep vocabulary to form high-scoring bingos and words. This is especially true for 2-letter and 3-letter words as the give out potential for overlap plays and score high points

- Strategic Play

It's crucial to maximize points while minimizing opportunities for opponents.

- Board Awareness

Effective placement of words to utilize premium squares (double/triple word/letter scores) can significantly impact the game. You also have to be aware of the words on the board and in endgame, your opponent's rack in order to block your opponent's best plays.

- Anagramming

Quickly identifying potential word combinations from scrambled letters is essential.

Our website provides resources such as variety of flashcards for multiple word lists which will be useful for certain situations such as shedding vowels an "Q"s and also an anagram trainer where you aim to find as many words as possible in a given rack. You can find them in the buttons below.
